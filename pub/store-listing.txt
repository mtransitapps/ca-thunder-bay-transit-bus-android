Full description:
This application adds Thunder Bay Transit buses information to MonTransit BETA.

This app contains the buses schedule and the latest news from @tbaytransit on Twitter.

BETA: Thanks for your patience while the last features are being added and the last bugs are being fixed.

Thunder Bay Transit serves Thunder Bay in Ontario, Canada.

Once this application is installed, the MonTransit BETA app will display buses information (schedule...).

This application only has a temporary icon and no screen: download the MonTransit BETA app (free) in the "More ..." section bellow or by following this Google Play link http://goo.gl/pCk5mV

You can install this application on the SD card but it is not recommended.

The information comes from the GTFS file provided by Thunder Bay Transit.
 http://www.thunderbay.ca/Living/Getting_Around/Thunder_Bay_Transit/Developers_-_Open_Data.htm

This application is free and open-source:
https://github.com/mtransitapps/ca-thunder-bay-transit-bus-android

This app is not related with Thunder Bay Transit .
