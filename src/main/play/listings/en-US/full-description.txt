This application adds Thunder Bay Transit buses information to MonTransit.

This app contains the buses schedule as well as real time service alerts and the latest news from @tbaytransit on Twitter.

Thunder Bay Transit buses serve Thunder Bay in Ontario, Canada.

Once this application is installed, the MonTransit app will display buses information (schedule...).

This application only has a temporary icon: download the MonTransit app (free) in the "More ..." section bellow or by following this Google Play link https://goo.gl/pCk5mV

You can install this application on the SD card but it is not recommended.

The information comes from the GTFS file provided by Thunder Bay Transit.
https://www.thunderbay.ca/en/city-services/developers---open-data.aspx

This application is free and open-source:
https://github.com/mtransitapps/ca-thunder-bay-transit-bus-android

This app is not related with Thunder Bay Transit.

Permissions:
- Other: required to read real time service alerts from nextlift.ca and to read news (Twitter)
